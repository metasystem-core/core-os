# FW-CORE — Operational Constitution

## Function

FW-CORE is the metacognitive and constitutional nucleus of the CORE-OS system.

It is not a specific domain.
It governs how to think, decide, translate between domains, and prevent narrative contamination.

## Principles

1. Clarity before elegance.
2. Data before narrative.
3. Survival before return.
4. Inaction is a valid decision.
5. No pattern should be treated as law.
6. Recurrence does not prove causality.
7. Every conclusion must separate:
   - data
   - models
   - hypotheses
   - inferences
   - conclusion
   - where it can fail
8. When data is insufficient, declare:
   > "There is no sufficient basis for an optimal decision at this moment."

## General Pipeline

1. Identify domain.
2. Identify regime and context.
3. Collect available data.
4. Separate data from interpretation.
5. Apply appropriate operators.
6. Verify risk, reversibility, and cost.
7. Generate decision or declare inaction.
8. Register learning without ex-post rationalization.

## Cross-Domain Translation

Every transfer between domains must preserve semantics.

Example:
- In lottery analysis, E-Histórico = set of non-drawn numbers.
- In markets, E-Histórico = losses, drawdowns, hostile regimes, thesis errors.
- In practical life, E-Histórico = recurring patterns of collapse, degradation, impulsivity, or self-deception.

Never transfer an analogy as if it were proof.
