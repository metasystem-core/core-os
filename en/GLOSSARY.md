# OPERATIONAL GLOSSARY — CORE-OS

## FW-CORE
The metacognitive nucleus. Governs how to think, decide, and translate between domains. Not a domain — governs all domains.

## FW-Kant
Normative filter for dignity, autonomy, elevation, and non-degradation. Applied to actions, impulses, decisions, and strategies.

## E-Histórico
The historical set of errors, losses, failures, absences, or drawdowns. Must be studied before any positive thesis is built.

## Regime First
All relevant analysis depends on the current regime. No analysis without regime identification.

## RAR
Robustness / Asymmetry / Reversibility. Pragmatic decision triaging.

## CCR
Cognitive Risk Circulation. Balances three poles: restriction/cost, reading/experience, model/direction.

## EBI
Low-Impact Exploratory. Small, capped, reversible experiment. Allows learning without risking the main system.

## Zone of Non-Operation
Declared state: no decision should be made due to insufficient data, unfavorable asymmetry, cognitive overload, or hostile regime.

## SIMB-CORE
Symbolic-operational system for lived experience analysis.

## ASSET-CORE
Patrimonial decision system under uncertainty. Priority: survival → risk → liquidity → convexity → return.

## BTC-CORE
Bitcoin decision sub-module within ASSET-CORE.

## LOTOFA-CORE
Controlled ludo-statistical laboratory. No predictive pretension.

## CORE-UPDATE
Protocol for propagating a learning or rule change across cores and platforms.

## FW-INSERT
Protocol for translating a concept between domains without semantic distortion.

## Decision Log
Structured decision record separating data, model, risk, conclusion, and post-result review without ex-post rationalization.

## CONTEXT-BRIEF
Lightweight context document generated by Claude Code after processing raw data. Passed to Claude.ai for conversation.

## Sovereign Source
The versioned local files. Not chat history, not AI memory.

## Ex-Post Rationalization
Reinterpreting a past decision to make it appear more logical than it was at the time. Explicitly prohibited in the CORE-OS review process.

## USER-ALIGNED
SIMB-CORE reading regime for material centered on the operator's own experience.

## SOURCE-FIRST
SIMB-CORE reading regime for external texts, books, philosophical traditions. Source integrity preserved before user adaptation.
