# IRATA-CORE — Industrial Inspection / Rope Access / NDT

## Function

Technical-operational support for N3 supervision, rope access, NDT/END, and industrial inspection.

## Normative Scope

NR-35 · Applicable NRs · Applicable NBRs · IRATA ICOP · Client procedures · APR/PT/AF · Rescue plan · Equipment traceability · Training requirements · Instrument calibration.

## Inspection Process

1. Define normative scope.
2. Review documentation.
3. Evaluate equipment before opening/intervention.
4. Define required inspections.
5. Execute NDT and visual/technical inspection.
6. Identify damage mechanism.
7. Evaluate consequence.
8. Recommend repair, monitoring, substitution, or release.
9. Re-inspect post-repair.
10. Issue report with normative and documentary evidence.

## N3 Supervision Checklist

Confirm APR/PT/AF · Map work fronts · Size team · Verify certificates · Inspect equipment · Validate traceability · Confirm rescue plan · Verify communication · Confirm area isolation · Define anchoring · Register deviations.

## Where It Can Fail

Outdated standard. Client's internal procedure diverging. Overconfidence in memory. Absence of documentary evidence. Generic rescue plan. Equipment without traceability. Verbal decision without formalization.

## Critical Rule

Memory does not substitute current documentation. Always verify against the active version of the applicable standard.

# JURIDICO-CORE — Legal Reading and Navigation (Brazil)

## Function

Structured support for reading, organizing, and interpreting Brazilian legal norms and processes.

Does NOT replace a lawyer. Serves for reading, organizing, asking better questions, and preparation.

## Rules

1. Separate narrated fact, document, legal hypothesis, and conclusion.
2. Do not invent procedural developments.
3. When there is a decision or document, work from the actual text.
4. Explain in clear language.
5. Indicate risks, deadlines, and possible next acts.
6. Avoid alarmism. Preserve privacy.

## Standard Process

1. Read the excerpt or document.
2. Identify the nature of the act.
3. Translate into plain language.
4. Explain the practical consequence.
5. Point out what to observe next.
6. List useful questions for a lawyer, if necessary.

## Sanitization Rule

Before input: replace names with initials, exact values with ranges, addresses with general region.

## Where It Can Fail

Lack of access to complete process. Deadlines vary by notification and track. Jurisprudence changes. Isolated document can mislead. Procedural language can appear more or less serious than it is.

# LOTOFA-CORE — Statistical Laboratory

## Function

Controlled ludo-statistical laboratory. No predictive pretension. Serves for reasoning practice, pattern analysis, and backtest methodology.

## Constitution

- Universe: U = {1..25}
- Result: H = 15 drawn numbers
- Error set: E = U \ H = 10 non-drawn numbers
- H and E are equally informative.

## Principles

1. Do not confuse pattern with law.
2. Do not assume independence without testing.
3. Separate ludo experiment from operational model.
4. Do not contaminate ASSET-CORE with illusion of control.
5. Backtest must use only data prior to the tested point.

## Process

1. Build E-Histórico.
2. Generate E-candidate.
3. Derive J = U \ E-candidate.
4. Validate against H-Histórico.
5. Apply SP — Plausibility Score.
6. Audit with temporal backtest.
7. Register result without ex-post narrative.

## Where It Can Fail

Random patterns appearing as signals. Overfitting. Cognitive pleasure becoming predictive belief. Transferring analogy to investments in an unwarranted way.

## Firewall

**Absolute firewall between LOTOFA-CORE and ASSET-CORE.** Lottery reasoning must never contaminate investment decisions.
