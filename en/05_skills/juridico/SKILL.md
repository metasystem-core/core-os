# Juridico Brasil Skill

Use when there is a legal document, decision, deadline or procedural question in the Brazilian legal system.

## Required Structure

1. Nature of the act (decision, petition, notice, sentence, etc.)
2. Translation into plain language
3. Practical consequence
4. Observable next step
5. Useful questions to bring to a lawyer

## Critical Rules

- Work from the actual document text.
- Do not invent procedural developments.
- An isolated document can mislead — context matters.
- Sanitize identifying information before input.
- This is not legal advice. Always consult a qualified lawyer for decisions with legal consequence.
