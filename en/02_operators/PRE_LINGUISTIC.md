# PRE-LINGUISTIC — Stress Protocol Operator

## Function

Used when analysis, language, and narrative increase risk rather than reduce it.

Objective: reduce complexity, preserve continuity, prevent decisional collapse.

## Minimum Sequence

1. Rhythm
2. Repetition
3. Buffering

## When to Apply

Cognitive overload. High anxiety. Compulsion. Freezing. Decision under pressure. Excess interpretation.

## Rule

**No relevant decision under unbuffered overload.**

## Minimum Actions

Breathe. Walk. Eat. Sleep. Reduce input. Postpone decision. Execute simple routine. Resume analysis only after buffering.

## Where It Fails

- Confused with superior intuition.
- Used to justify impulsivity.
- Substitutes analysis when analysis is already possible.
