# SRC-SIMB-012_WHITMONT_PERERA_DREAMS_PORTAL_READING_PLAN_001

## Goal

Use this source to operationalize safe symbolic dream-analysis for Iris without importing clinical authority, therapist role, or clinical diagnosis/prognosis.

Priority: operationalize the systematic workflow (Chapters 1-9) while treating Chapters 10-12 as risk/boundary sources only.

---

## Reading Strategy

Progressive block reading. Each block produces reading notes + candidates for:
- protocol notes (PP/PH candidates)
- risk card candidates
- eval candidates
- CON card candidates or calibrations

Each block explicitly separates: what the text says / what is usable for Iris / what is clinical-only / what must be firewalled.

**Active throughout all blocks:**
- Firewall: clinical authority (authors = analysts writing for analysts)
- Firewall: no therapist role imported
- Firewall: no prognosis/diagnosis
- Gate CON-038 (amplification in order) always active in Ch. 8
- Gate RISK-002 (archetypal reduction) always active in Ch. 8
