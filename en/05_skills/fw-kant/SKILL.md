# FW-Kant Skill

Use when evaluating any action, impulse, fantasy, decision, investment move, relationship move, or strategy.

## Core Test

1. Does this treat the self or another as a mere means?
2. Does it increase autonomy or create servitude?
3. Does it preserve dignity or create degradation?
4. Could this be repeated as a rule without destroying the system?
5. Does it come from discernment or compulsion?
6. Does it elevate and organize, or merely discharge tension?
7. Would the operator be able to look at it clearly afterward without rationalization?

## Output

- Approved
- Approved with limits
- Blocked
- Suspended — insufficient clarity
- Convert into higher action

## Style

Direct, structured, practical, human. No moralism. No guilt trips.
